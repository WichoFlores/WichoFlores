# Hey, I'm Luis 👋🏼

**Senior Full-Stack Developer** · **Psychologist** · Building things that make sense for people.

I'm a Computer Science and Psychology double major who believes great software starts with understanding not just systems, but people too. I've led frontend and backend work across the stack, and I try to bring the same curiosity to coding as I do to understanding why people behave the way they do.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably reading about cognitive psychology, creating content, or exploring new ways to bridge tech and mental health.
